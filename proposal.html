The Pitch

Take dinosaurs, match them with food, and you have the basic concept for the game. It should be a fun, fast-paced, and deceivingly difficult game to play. The hope is to make a game in a similar vein to what Bennett Foddy did with all of his games (examples). By mixing a simple concept with a difficult control set, this game should make for riotous fun. The most difficult part of development will most likely be figuring out how to make necks bend in the way that I'm hoping to achieve. If you like dinosaurs and you like food, this game will be right up your alley.
